Bomb Buddies

Bomb Buddies is an augmented reality (AR), cooperative, mobile puzzle game. In Bomb Buddies, solve puzzles, crack codes, and test your dexterity with a friend...but watch out! Everything is not as it seems when a nefarious hacker gets involved. When the stakes are high can your skills still hold up to the test?

Bomb Buddies was revealed during the Advanced Games Projects (AGP) Spotlight Show at USC Games Expo 2023. It is made in Unity and is available on the IOS App Store.

Key Features
Play multiple exciting minigames each with different AR features with your buddy, or anyone you can find!
Experience the fully voiced Story Mode to find out the (maybe) tragic backstory of the “evil” Hacker!
Try Party Mode to return to your favorite minigames whenever you want for some goofy party fun!
Jam to juicy audio tracks and stylized visuals, all fully original, fresh from the team.
