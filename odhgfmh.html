One Day, Horns Grew From My Head.

One Day, Horns Grew From My Head is a top-down narrative puzzle game. that follows a boy named Y who, upon waking up in a strange room with no memories, must find the passwords to all his online journal entries in order to rediscover his identity and the circumstances leading him to this room. A new room can be accessed by the one door in the strange room each time you unlock a journal entry. Solve the puzzle in each room in order to complete this feat.

One Day, Horns Grew From My Head is a fan-project inspired by the artist Tomorrow X Together and is made in Unity and is available on Itch.io.
