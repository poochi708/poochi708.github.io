Science Splice

Science Splice is a light-hearted virtual escape room experience for 2 or more players. In Science Splice, players play as a lab intern that gets split into two (right brain and left brain) from an accident gone wrong. Cooperate with the other player(s) to solve the puzzles that you each only see half of and put yourself back together using the power of science! Or else, remain split into two forever!

Science Splice was available as an online playable experience during the pandemic in Spring 2021. It is built in Telescape. The Escape Roomer review! ESCAPETHEROOMers review!
