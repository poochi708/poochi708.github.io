Steampun(k)

Steampun(k) is an in-person escape room. In Steampun(k), players are invited to a reading of Punkie Steamweld's will. They then dive into an immersive steampunk-era room where they play as one of Punkie Steamweld's loved ones to solve a variety of puzzles and potentially inherit his fortune.

Steampun(k) was available to play for two weeks in November 2022.

Key Features
4 multi-step unique puzzles
Immersive experience with character identities and costumes
Steampunk era decor
In-room actor to give hints
Prisoner's dilemma twist at the end and custom giftshop of prizes for winners
Many, many puns about gears, steam, and punks

Ghoul Grub

Ghoul Grub is a paranormal puzzle scavenger hunt where players act as delivery drivers and solve puzzles to help monsters sort out their dishes to be customer-ready.

Ghoul Grub was an in-person experience with various stations across USC's campus that was available to play in October 2021.

Key Features

Spooky halloween puzzles and decorations

The King's Labyrinth

The King's Labyrinth is a first-person, 3D puzzle maze. Players enter an evil king's mysterious white labyrinth in search of their missing sibling. They solve puzzles, speak with characters, and look for objects to unlock the truth.

The King's Labyrinth was built in Unity and is available to play on Itch.io.

The Noble House of Black

The Noble House of Black is a Harry Potter inspired escape room concept. Players play as mid-level death eaters serving the Voldemort in 1979, sent on an assignment to retrieve his mysterious artifact stolen by former traitorous death eater Regulus Black. However, Regulus set up a trap! Players have one hour to escape before they are arrested by the Order of the Phoenix.

Key Features
4 unique locations (2 initially hidden)
6 Playable Character options and costumes
6 audio, visual, and environmentally-interactive puzzles
